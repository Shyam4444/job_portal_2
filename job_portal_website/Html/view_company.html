About Company

International Business Machines Corporation (IBM) is a renowned multinational technology company with its headquarters in Armonk, New York, USA. The company operates in various segments, including cloud computing, artificial intelligence, data analytics, cybersecurity, and enterprise software solutions. IBM's extensive portfolio of products and services caters to diverse industries, including banking, healthcare, retail, telecommunications, and government sectors. With a rich history of innovation, IBM has pioneered many advancements in computing technology, including the development of the mainframe computer, the invention of the floppy disk, and the creation of the relational database. IBM places a strong emphasis on research and development, investing significantly in emerging technologies such as quantum computing and blockchain. Additionally, the company is committed to corporate responsibility, focusing on environmental sustainability, diversity and inclusion, and ethical business practices. IBM's global presence, technological expertise, and commitment to innovation make it a key player in shaping the future of enterprise computing and digital transformation.
